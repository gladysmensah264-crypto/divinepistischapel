About Us

Divine Pistis Chapel is a vibrant Christian ministry founded in Ghana by Rev. Lord Darlington Ayiku. We are dedicated to building faith, love, and spiritual empowerment through worship, prayer, and the Word of God. Our mission is to guide believers into a deeper relationship with Christ and impact our community for God’s Kingdom.
